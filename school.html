Alma Mater

This is my first time going back to school since leaving CSULB in spring of 2023. I graduated in 2023 with a degree in Business Marketing and have been working to attain my career goals ever since. During my time there, I felt like I really grew into myself. Originally, I didn't have any concrete plans for my future, but I went from a sheltered book worm to someone I feel can light up a room and connect with people through entertainment. One thing I wish I could have changed during my time there was planning out my courses better. I only took the courses necessary to graduate and missed a lot of opportunities to expand my skill set. I hope to rectify that as soon as possible with the courses I'm now taking at Cerritos College.
